# 03 Sales

## Purpose

This folder manages how PN_AI_SYSTEM turns trust, value, and relationships into revenue.

Sales is not pressure. Sales is the process of matching valuable capability with people who need results. The sales system should help clients, partners, and collaborators see how PN_AI_SYSTEM can help them reach more people, make more money, launch stronger products, or create cultural momentum.

The long-term aim is to sell and manage increasingly larger projects, including production, launch, marketing, AI systems, and growth projects capable of reaching 100 million yen scale.

## KPI

- Qualified leads added per week
- Sales conversations held per month
- Proposal-to-close conversion rate
- Average project value
- Monthly recurring revenue from retainers or ongoing systems
- Revenue generated for clients or partners
- Number of offers that create assets, not only service hours
- Pipeline value by stage
- Number of 10 million yen plus and 100 million yen potential opportunities identified

## Operating Procedures

1. Maintain a lead pipeline with status, source, need, budget, authority, timeline, and next step.
2. Segment leads into people, products, culture, companies, investors, and partners.
3. Use discovery calls to identify the economic outcome: more sales, larger audience, stronger launch, better positioning, or operational leverage.
4. Create proposals that show business outcome, project scope, timeline, deliverables, required decisions, and price.
5. Follow up with every qualified lead until closed, paused, or clearly disqualified.
6. Review the pipeline weekly and remove vague leads with no next step.
7. Convert repeated client needs into productized offers or templates.
8. Track revenue created for clients so case studies can prove economic value.
9. Build toward larger deals by improving proof, process, team capacity, and partner network.

## Required Assets

- `offers.md`: current offers, pricing logic, and outcomes
- `lead_pipeline.md`: active sales opportunities
- `proposal_template.md`: reusable proposal structure
- `discovery_questions.md`: sales questions focused on money, reach, and assets
- `followup_sequences.md`: email and message follow-up templates
- `case_studies.md`: proof of revenue, reach, and cultural impact
- `large_project_qualification.md`: criteria for 10 million yen and 100 million yen opportunities

## AI Automation Opportunities

- Draft proposals from discovery notes
- Score leads by fit, urgency, budget, and strategic value
- Generate follow-up messages based on conversation history
- Summarize sales calls into needs, objections, next steps, and offer recommendations
- Identify repeated client problems that can become products
- Forecast pipeline value and expected close dates
