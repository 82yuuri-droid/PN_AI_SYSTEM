# 09 Templates

## Purpose

This folder stores reusable templates for faster, cleaner execution.

Templates turn experience into assets. Every repeated document, brief, proposal, checklist, report, sales message, launch plan, or review should become a template. This reduces time spent recreating work and increases the quality of delivery.

Templates are part of the asset strategy: they make the business easier to operate, easier to teach, easier to delegate, and easier to scale.

## KPI

- Number of active templates
- Number of templates used per month
- Time saved through template usage
- Reduction in delivery errors or missing information
- Number of templates converted into products, lead magnets, or internal tools
- Template update frequency after real projects
- Percentage of repeated workflows supported by templates

## Operating Procedures

1. Create a template whenever the same format is used more than twice.
2. Keep templates simple, clear, and outcome-oriented.
3. Add instructions, required inputs, output standards, and examples where useful.
4. Review templates after every project that uses them.
5. Store templates by use case: sales, marketing, operations, products, recruiting, strategy, AI, and reporting.
6. Mark each template as draft, active, deprecated, or productizable.
7. Convert high-value templates into assets that can support sales, onboarding, training, or paid products.
8. Keep a template index so the right file can be found quickly.

## Required Assets

- `template_index.md`: list of all templates and use cases
- `brief_template.md`: project and creative brief
- `proposal_template.md`: sales proposal
- `meeting_notes_template.md`: structured meeting notes
- `launch_plan_template.md`: launch and campaign planning
- `case_study_template.md`: proof-of-work format
- `weekly_review_template.md`: operating review
- `ai_prompt_template.md`: standard prompt format

## AI Automation Opportunities

- Generate first drafts from templates and project inputs
- Suggest template improvements from completed work
- Auto-fill proposals, briefs, reports, and checklists
- Detect missing information in template-based documents
- Convert successful documents into reusable templates
- Maintain a searchable template index
