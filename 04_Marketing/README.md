# 04 Marketing

## Purpose

This folder manages how PN_AI_SYSTEM earns attention, builds trust, and creates demand.

Marketing exists to help valuable people, products, and culture reach the people who need them. The system should show taste, proof, strategic thinking, and production capability. It should also create assets: content, case studies, frameworks, audience lists, media properties, and campaigns that compound over time.

Marketing should make the producer identity visible before a sales conversation begins.

## KPI

- Number of high-quality content assets published per week
- Audience growth by channel
- Newsletter or owned audience growth
- Inbound leads generated
- Case studies published
- Campaign reach, engagement, and conversion
- Number of valuable people, products, or cultural ideas amplified
- Number of content assets reused across multiple channels
- Reputation indicators: referrals, invitations, collaborations, media mentions

## Operating Procedures

1. Maintain a clear positioning document: who PN_AI_SYSTEM helps, what outcomes it creates, and why it is different.
2. Build a monthly content plan around producer thinking, case studies, market observations, and useful frameworks.
3. Publish proof of work regularly, including before-and-after examples, campaign breakdowns, and lessons learned.
4. Build an owned audience through a newsletter, community list, or direct relationship database.
5. Run campaigns for important launches, not random posts.
6. Repurpose every major idea into multiple formats: essay, short post, deck, video outline, sales asset, and internal playbook.
7. Track which content creates conversations, leads, and opportunities.
8. Turn repeated marketing messages into durable assets, such as frameworks and signature concepts.

## Required Assets

- `positioning.md`: core public identity and differentiation
- `brand_voice.md`: language standards and tone
- `content_calendar.md`: weekly and monthly publishing plan
- `campaigns.md`: active and past campaign plans
- `case_studies.md`: public proof of outcomes
- `audience_map.md`: who the work is trying to reach
- `distribution_channels.md`: owned, earned, paid, and partner channels
- `signature_frameworks.md`: reusable ideas that make the brand memorable

## AI Automation Opportunities

- Turn project notes into case studies and social posts
- Generate content calendars from strategic priorities
- Repurpose long-form content into channel-specific formats
- Analyze which posts generate leads and conversations
- Monitor market and cultural trends relevant to producer work
- Draft campaign plans, audience maps, and distribution checklists
