# 01 Vision

## Purpose

This folder defines the long-term direction of PN_AI_SYSTEM.

The vision is to become a producer who helps valuable people, products, and culture reach the people who need them. This means identifying under-amplified value, shaping it into clear stories and offers, and building systems that help others create money, opportunity, and cultural impact.

This is also the home for the 100 million yen ambition: becoming capable of creating, leading, and managing projects with budgets, revenue targets, partnerships, and outcomes at that scale.

## KPI

- Clear written mission reviewed monthly
- 1-year, 3-year, and 10-year targets documented and updated quarterly
- Number of high-value people, products, or cultural projects identified
- Number of assets created from the vision: essays, frameworks, decks, offers, products
- Progress toward 100 million yen project capability: project size, budget handled, revenue influenced, team size managed
- Alignment score for active projects: does each project help valuable things reach the right people?

## Operating Procedures

1. Review the mission every month and rewrite it only when the business has meaningfully evolved.
2. Maintain a `north_star.md` file that explains the main direction in plain language.
3. Keep a `producer_identity.md` file that defines what kind of producer you are becoming.
4. Use a quarterly vision review to decide what to continue, stop, and begin.
5. Evaluate every major opportunity against four filters: value, reach, money system, and asset potential.
6. Keep a visible 100 million yen project map showing required skills, relationships, proof, capital, team, and operating systems.
7. Convert abstract beliefs into practical standards that guide daily decisions.

## Required Assets

- `mission.md`: the core mission in one page
- `values.md`: standards for money, culture, relationships, and execution
- `north_star.md`: the current long-term direction
- `producer_identity.md`: personal operating identity and positioning
- `100m_yen_project_map.md`: roadmap to producing 100 million yen projects
- `opportunity_filters.md`: criteria for deciding what to pursue
- `vision_review_log.md`: monthly and quarterly reflections

- Identify repeated themes across journals, project notes, and strategy docs
