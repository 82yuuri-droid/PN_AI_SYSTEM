# 05 Recruiting

## Purpose

This folder manages the people system behind PN_AI_SYSTEM.

To produce larger projects, the business must move from solo execution to coordinated capability. Recruiting includes employees, contractors, advisors, partners, creators, specialists, and AI-augmented collaborators. The goal is to build teams that can help valuable people, products, and culture reach more people while creating measurable economic outcomes.

This folder is especially important for becoming capable of managing 100 million yen projects, because that scale requires trust, delegation, expert networks, and clear operating roles.

## KPI

- Number of qualified collaborators in the network
- Time to find the right specialist for a project
- Number of active role scorecards
- Contractor quality rating after each project
- Percentage of projects with clear owner and backup owner
- Number of advisors or senior operators available for large projects
- Team capacity for larger project budgets and timelines
- Revenue per collaborator or project team

## Operating Procedures

1. Maintain a role map that shows current and future capability needs.
2. Separate roles into core team, contractors, advisors, partners, and AI agents.
3. Create scorecards for each important role before recruiting.
4. Keep a collaborator pipeline with skills, past work, rates, availability, trust level, and best-fit project type.
5. Use trial projects before relying on a collaborator for critical work.
6. After every project, rate collaborators on quality, speed, communication, ownership, and judgment.
7. Build a bench for high-leverage areas: design, video, writing, sales, operations, AI, finance, legal, media, and partnerships.
8. For large opportunities, assemble a project team plan before committing to the scope.

## Required Assets

- `role_map.md`: capability needs by stage of business
- `collaborator_pipeline.md`: trusted people and potential hires
- `hiring_scorecards.md`: criteria for evaluating each role
- `contractor_briefs.md`: reusable briefs for external contributors
- `advisor_network.md`: senior people who can support large decisions
- `project_team_templates.md`: team structures for different project types
- `post_project_reviews.md`: collaborator performance records

## AI Automation Opportunities

- Match project needs with collaborator profiles
- Draft role descriptions and scorecards
- Summarize interviews and trial projects
- Track collaborator performance over time
- Suggest team structures for different project scopes
- Identify recruiting gaps based on the project roadmap
