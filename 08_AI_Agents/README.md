# 08 AI Agents

## Purpose

This folder manages AI agents, prompts, automations, and workflows.

AI agents are leverage systems. They should help PN_AI_SYSTEM research faster, think clearer, produce assets, support sales, analyze opportunities, manage operations, and increase the ability to run bigger projects. AI should not replace judgment. It should extend capacity while keeping human taste, ethics, and strategic control at the center.

The goal is to build an AI-assisted producer system that can help others make money and reach more people with less wasted effort.

## KPI

- Number of documented AI agents
- Hours saved per week through AI workflows
- Number of assets created or improved with AI support
- Quality score of AI outputs after human review
- Number of repeatable workflows automated
- Reduction in time from idea to proposal, brief, campaign, or product spec
- Number of business functions supported by AI agents
- Error rate or rework rate for AI-assisted outputs

## Operating Procedures

1. Maintain an agent registry with role, purpose, inputs, outputs, tools, owner, and review requirements.
2. Define clear prompts for recurring work before automating it.
3. Require human review for strategy, money, legal, reputation, and final client-facing outputs.
4. Test agents on real examples and keep only workflows that improve speed, quality, or consistency.
5. Connect AI workflows to business outcomes: revenue, reach, asset creation, or operational reliability.
6. Keep versioned prompt templates for important agents.
7. Review AI workflows monthly and remove outdated or low-quality automations.
8. Design agents for the 100 million yen project path: research, stakeholder briefs, project reports, risk tracking, sales support, and launch management.

## Required Assets

- `agent_registry.md`: list of all AI agents and responsibilities
- `prompt_library.md`: reusable prompts
- `automation_map.md`: workflows and triggers
- `agent_workflows.md`: step-by-step AI operating procedures
- `evaluation_criteria.md`: quality standards for AI output
- `human_review_rules.md`: what must be checked by a person
- `ai_tool_stack.md`: tools, models, integrations, and costs
- `agent_roadmap.md`: next agents to build

## AI Automation Opportunities

- Research agent for market, audience, and competitor analysis
- Sales agent for lead scoring, proposals, and follow-ups
- Marketing agent for content repurposing and campaign planning
- Product agent for specs, feedback analysis, and launch checklists
- Operations agent for weekly reviews, dashboards, risks, and SOPs
- Recruiting agent for role matching and collaborator summaries
- Knowledge agent for turning notes into organized memory
