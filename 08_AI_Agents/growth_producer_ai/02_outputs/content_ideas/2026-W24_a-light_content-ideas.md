# A-LIGHT Content Ideas

## Goal

Increase talent applications and brand awareness.

## 10 Priority Content Ideas

## 1. "What is A-LIGHT?"

Format:

- Short video or carousel

Hook:

- "A-LIGHT is not just a company name. Here is what we actually do for talent."

Purpose:

- Awareness and brand clarity

CTA:

- "If this sounds like the environment you are looking for, check the application link."

## 2. "A Day Inside A-LIGHT"

Format:

- Short video

Hook:

- "What does a normal day at A-LIGHT actually look like?"

Purpose:

- Culture and candidate imagination

CTA:

- "Save this if you are considering applying."

## 3. "Who Fits A-LIGHT?"

Format:

- Carousel

Hook:

- "You may be a fit for A-LIGHT if these 5 things sound like you."

Purpose:

- Candidate self-selection

CTA:

- "DM us or apply if this feels like you."

## 4. "Why Our People Joined"

Format:

- Interview clips

Hook:

- "We asked our team why they chose A-LIGHT."

Purpose:

- Trust and emotional proof

CTA:

- "Want to join this kind of environment? See the application details."

## 5. "Application Process Explained"

Format:

- Carousel or story highlight

Hook:

- "Thinking about applying? Here is what happens after you send your application."

Purpose:

- Reduce uncertainty

CTA:

- "Start here when you are ready."

## 6. "Candidate FAQ: Do I Need Experience?"

Format:

- Short video / Q&A post

Hook:

- "Do you need experience to apply to A-LIGHT? The honest answer is..."

Purpose:

- Objection handling

CTA:

- "Send us your question or check the application page."

## 7. "Skills That Grow Here"

Format:

- Carousel

Hook:

- "These are the skills people build through A-LIGHT."

Purpose:

- Growth motivation

CTA:

- "If you want to grow these skills, apply or ask us a question."

## 8. "Behind the Scenes of Talent Support"

Format:

- Short video

Hook:

- "What kind of support happens behind the scenes?"

Purpose:

- Proof of care and development

CTA:

- "Follow for more behind-the-scenes stories."

## 9. "My First Step With A-LIGHT"

Format:

- Story/interview series

Hook:

- "Everyone starts somewhere. Here was my first step."

Purpose:

- Make applying feel less intimidating

CTA:

- "Your first step can start with a simple application."

## 10. "This Month's Open Opportunities"

Format:

- Monthly recurring post

Hook:

- "A-LIGHT is currently looking for people who want to..."

Purpose:

- Direct conversion

CTA:

- "Apply from the link in profile by [date]."

## Recommended Weekly Mix

| Week | Awareness | Trust | Conversion |
| --- | --- | --- | --- |
| Week 1 | What is A-LIGHT? | Why Our People Joined | Application Process Explained |
| Week 2 | Skills That Grow Here | A Day Inside A-LIGHT | Candidate FAQ |
| Week 3 | Behind the Scenes | My First Step With A-LIGHT | Who Fits A-LIGHT? |
| Week 4 | Open Opportunities | People Story Recap | Application CTA |

## Best 3 Ideas To Produce First

1. What is A-LIGHT?
2. Who Fits A-LIGHT?
3. Application Process Explained

Reason:

These three assets create the minimum recruiting funnel: brand clarity, candidate self-selection, and conversion path.
