# A-LIGHT Proposal Summary

## Proposal Title

A-LIGHT Recruiting SNS Growth System

## Client Situation

A-LIGHT wants to increase talent applications and brand awareness. Existing PN context suggests A-LIGHT already has recruiting SNS planning assets, including企画シート,ヒアリングシート, andワンページメモ references.

The opportunity is to turn those assets into a repeatable recruiting SNS system that increases awareness, trust, and applications.

## Business Problem

A-LIGHT likely has valuable opportunities and culture, but candidates may not clearly understand:

- What A-LIGHT does
- Who fits
- What the work/culture feels like
- What growth looks like
- How to apply
- Why applying now is worth it

If these questions are not answered through content and application flow, brand awareness may increase without producing enough qualified applications.

## Desired Outcome

- Increase monthly talent applications
- Increase qualified application rate
- Improve candidate understanding before interviews
- Build stronger recruiting brand awareness
- Create reusable recruiting content assets
- Build a monthly reporting and improvement loop

## Recommended PN Approach

PN should deliver a 90-day recruiting SNS growth project.

The project combines:

- Recruiting message strategy
- SNS content pillars
- Short-form video and carousel concepts
- Application CTA and profile structure improvement
- Monthly KPI reporting
- Candidate FAQ and objection-handling content
- Improvement recommendations

## Scope

## Phase 1: Diagnostic and Strategy

Deliverables:

- Candidate persona map
- Recruiting message map
- SNS channel audit
- Profile and application path recommendations
- KPI dashboard design

## Phase 2: Content System

Deliverables:

- 5 content pillars
- 30-day content calendar
- 10 priority content ideas
- Candidate FAQ content plan
- Employee/talent story interview plan
- Application CTA templates

## Phase 3: Reporting and Improvement

Deliverables:

- Monthly performance report
- Content learning summary
- Application funnel analysis
- Next-month improvement plan
- Upsell or additional asset recommendations only if justified

## Timeline

Recommended initial term:

- 90 days

Suggested rhythm:

- Week 1: Diagnostic and strategy
- Week 2: Content calendar and production plan
- Weeks 3-8: Publishing and data collection
- Weeks 9-12: Improvement, reporting, and next-phase recommendation

## Success Metrics

Primary:

- Monthly talent applications
- Qualified applications
- Interviews booked
- Applications attributed to SNS

Secondary:

- Reach
- Profile visits
- Saves
- DM inquiries
- Link clicks
- Video completion rate
- Content approval time

## Client Responsibilities

A-LIGHT should provide:

- Hiring goals by role
- Ideal candidate profile
- Existing application flow
- Current SNS and recruiting metrics
- Access to current recruiting assets
- Interview access to employees/talent
- Timely content approval

## Risks and Assumptions

Assumptions:

- A-LIGHT can provide access to recruiting information and internal stories.
- A-LIGHT has or can create a clear application path.
- The team can approve content consistently.

Risks:

- Awareness grows but application path remains unclear.
- Content approval delays reduce momentum.
- No baseline KPI data makes improvement hard to prove.
- Lack of real people/story content makes recruiting brand generic.
