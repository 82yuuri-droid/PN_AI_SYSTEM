# A-LIGHT Client Analysis

## Client Context Summary

A-LIGHT is treated as a recruiting and brand-awareness client with existing PN context in Drive assets such as企画シート,ヒアリングシート, andワンページメモ. The likely business challenge is not simply "post more on SNS." The real challenge is making A-LIGHT visible, credible, and attractive to the right talent so more people apply and more qualified candidates move into interviews.

## Core Business Problem

A-LIGHT needs to increase talent applications while strengthening brand awareness among potential candidates.

The likely gap:

- Candidates do not understand what makes A-LIGHT attractive.
- The workplace, people, growth path, and culture may not be visible enough.
- Existing content may not clearly connect awareness to application behavior.
- Recruiting communication may be scattered across SNS, job postings, interviews, and candidate follow-up.

## Desired Outcome

- More qualified talent applications
- Stronger candidate awareness of A-LIGHT
- Higher trust before application or interview
- Better candidate understanding of culture, expectations, and growth opportunities
- A repeatable recruiting SNS system that can continue beyond one campaign

## Candidate Journey Diagnosis

| Stage | Candidate Question | A-LIGHT Content Job |
| --- | --- | --- |
| Awareness | "Who is A-LIGHT?" | Make the company visible and memorable |
| Interest | "What kind of people work there?" | Show people, roles, values, and real work |
| Trust | "Can I imagine myself there?" | Show daily reality, growth, support, and credibility |
| Application | "Should I apply now?" | Reduce uncertainty and make next action clear |
| Interview | "Am I a good fit?" | Prepare candidates with expectations and culture |

## Deal / Project Fit

Fit score: 4 out of 5.

Reason:

- Clear recruiting outcome
- Strong fit with PN's SNS, video, recruiting, and AI operating system strengths
- Existing client/project context appears to exist
- Content can become reusable recruiting assets

Missing information prevents a score of 5:

Best-fit offer:
