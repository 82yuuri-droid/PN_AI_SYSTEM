# 07 Operations

## Purpose

This folder manages the repeatable systems that keep PN_AI_SYSTEM running.

Operations turns ambition into reliable execution. It includes planning rhythms, project management, finance, reporting, delivery workflows, dashboards, and standard operating procedures. Strong operations are required to manage larger budgets, multiple stakeholders, and 100 million yen scale projects without chaos.

Operations should make the business easier to run, easier to delegate, and easier to improve.

## KPI

- Weekly review completion rate
- Projects delivered on time
- Budget variance by project
- Monthly revenue, expenses, profit, and cash runway
- Number of documented standard operating procedures
- Number of repeated tasks automated or delegated
- Project margin and delivery efficiency
- Decision turnaround time
- Large project readiness score: budget control, reporting, team ownership, risk management

## Operating Procedures

1. Run a weekly review covering priorities, pipeline, active projects, money, blockers, and next actions.
2. Run a monthly review covering revenue, expenses, assets created, audience growth, project outcomes, and lessons learned.
3. Maintain a project dashboard with owner, status, deadline, budget, next step, risk, and KPI.
4. Document any process that repeats more than three times.
5. Use project briefs before starting work and retrospectives after finishing work.
6. Track money carefully: revenue, cost, margin, unpaid invoices, cash commitments, and expected future income.
7. Create risk logs for larger projects, including scope, budget, legal, stakeholder, timeline, and quality risks.
8. Improve one operational bottleneck every week.

## Required Assets

- `weekly_review.md`: weekly operating rhythm
- `monthly_review.md`: monthly business review
- `project_dashboard.md`: active projects and status
- `finance_tracker.md`: revenue, expenses, profit, and cash
- `sops.md`: standard operating procedures
- `risk_register.md`: project and business risks
- `project_brief_template.md`: required project start document
- `retrospective_log.md`: lessons from completed work

## AI Automation Opportunities

- Generate weekly and monthly review summaries
- Detect overdue tasks, missing owners, and project risks
- Create project status reports from notes and task files
- Summarize financial data into plain-language insights
- Draft SOPs from repeated workflows
- Forecast capacity, deadlines, and budget risk for larger projects
