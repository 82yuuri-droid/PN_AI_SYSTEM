# 06 Products

## Purpose

This folder manages products, services, packages, and produced assets.

Products are how PN_AI_SYSTEM moves beyond trading time. A product can be a service package, AI workflow, launch system, course, template, media property, community, tool, or produced cultural project. Every product should help valuable people, products, or culture reach more people, make money, or create durable leverage.

The product system should create assets that can be reused, sold, licensed, scaled, or turned into larger projects.

## KPI

- Number of productized offers created
- Revenue by product or offer
- Gross margin by product
- Number of reusable assets produced
- Customer or client outcome metrics
- Time required to deliver each product
- Product repeatability score
- Number of products that can scale without equal time increase
- Number of products with 10 million yen plus or 100 million yen potential

## Operating Procedures

1. Keep a product roadmap with active, planned, testing, and retired products.
2. Define each product by target user, problem, outcome, deliverables, price, delivery method, and success metric.
3. Start with the simplest useful version before building complexity.
4. Turn repeated service work into productized systems.
5. Document delivery steps so the product can be delegated or automated.
6. Collect feedback after every delivery and update the product spec.
7. Review product economics monthly: price, margin, delivery time, demand, and strategic value.
8. Build products that create money systems for others, such as launch systems, sales assets, content engines, AI operations, or partnership models.

## Required Assets

- `product_roadmap.md`: current and future products
- `service_packages.md`: productized services and pricing
- `product_specs.md`: detailed product definitions
- `launch_checklists.md`: launch steps for each product
- `feedback_log.md`: user and client feedback
- `asset_inventory.md`: reusable assets created by each product
- `pricing_model.md`: pricing logic and margin targets
- `scale_plan.md`: path from custom service to repeatable product
