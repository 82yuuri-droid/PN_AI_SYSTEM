# Business Model

## Mission

Help valuable people, products, and culture reach more people through strategy, storytelling, production, and distribution.

## Who I Serve

### People

Creators, founders, experts, artists, educators, and operators with meaningful work but limited reach.

### Products

Useful, thoughtful, or culturally relevant products that need clearer positioning, stronger launch strategy, or better audience connection.

### Culture

Ideas, communities, places, movements, and creative work that deserve translation, amplification, and sustainable attention.

## Core Offers

### 1. Positioning and Narrative

Clarify the essence of a person, product, or project.

Deliverables may include:

- Positioning statement
- Audience map
- Brand narrative
- Messaging pillars
- Content angles
- Launch story

### 2. Launch and Campaign Production

Turn a valuable thing into a coordinated public moment.

Deliverables may include:

- Launch plan
- Campaign concept
- Content calendar
- Partnership list
- Media and creator outreach
- Event or digital activation plan

### 3. Content and Distribution System

Build repeatable ways for the right audience to discover and trust the work.

Deliverables may include:

- Editorial strategy
- Short-form and long-form content system
- Newsletter or community plan
- Social channel strategy
- Repurposing workflow
- Performance review rhythm

### 4. Producer Retainer

Ongoing strategic and execution support for select clients or projects.

Possible scope:

- Monthly strategy sessions
- Campaign planning
- Content direction
- Partnership development
- Launch support
- Audience growth review

## Revenue Streams

- Fixed-fee strategy projects
- Launch or campaign packages
- Monthly producer retainers
- Revenue share on selected products or launches
- Advisory equity for high-conviction startups or cultural ventures
- Workshops for teams, creators, or founders

## Differentiation

My advantage is the combination of taste, care, execution, and audience thinking.

I am not simply a marketer, consultant, or content person. I am a producer: someone who sees the value, shapes the frame, gathers the pieces, and helps the work reach the right people.

## Success Metrics

- Audience growth for clients and projects
- Quality of opportunities created
- Revenue or conversion lift from launches
- Strength of partnerships formed
- Repeat clients and referrals
- Portfolio reputation
- Cultural relevance and long-term trust
