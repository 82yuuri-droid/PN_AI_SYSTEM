# 10 Knowledge Base

## Purpose

This folder stores accumulated knowledge, research, references, insights, and lessons learned.

The knowledge base is the memory layer of PN_AI_SYSTEM. It should capture what you learn about people, products, culture, money, AI, markets, distribution, production, and leadership. Knowledge becomes valuable when it is organized, reused, and converted into decisions, assets, products, or strategy.

This folder should make the business smarter every month.

## KPI

- Research notes added per week
- Number of insights converted into strategy, content, products, or templates
- Decision records created for major choices
- Number of project lessons captured after completion
- Retrieval speed: how quickly useful knowledge can be found
- Number of reusable frameworks created from research
- Number of knowledge assets that support sales, marketing, or product development

## Operating Procedures

1. Capture research notes from books, articles, conversations, projects, markets, and cultural observations.
2. Convert raw notes into structured summaries with source, date, key insight, use case, and next action.
3. Maintain a decision log for important strategic, financial, hiring, and product decisions.
4. After every project, write lessons learned and extract reusable principles.
5. Review the knowledge base monthly and identify ideas that should become content, products, templates, or strategic moves.
6. Keep reference material organized by topic, not only by date.
7. Connect knowledge to outcomes: reach, money systems, assets, operations, and 100 million yen project readiness.
8. Remove or archive stale information that no longer helps decision-making.

## Required Assets

- `research_notes.md`: active research and summaries
- `book_notes.md`: lessons from books and long-form learning
- `market_references.md`: markets, categories, trends, and examples
- `lessons_learned.md`: project and business lessons
- `decision_log.md`: important decisions and reasoning
- `frameworks.md`: reusable thinking models
- `people_products_culture_database.md`: valuable people, products, and cultural references
- `insight_to_asset_log.md`: tracking how knowledge becomes assets
