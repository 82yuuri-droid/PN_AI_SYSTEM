# 02 Strategy

## Purpose

This folder turns the vision into choices.

Strategy decides where PN_AI_SYSTEM will focus, what markets and communities matter, which people and products deserve amplification, and how to build systems that create revenue for others. The goal is not to chase every opportunity. The goal is to choose the right arenas where taste, production ability, AI leverage, and distribution can create outsized results.

Strategy should always connect to asset creation. If an activity only trades time and does not create reusable knowledge, relationships, data, templates, media, products, or equity, it needs a strong reason to exist.

## KPI

- Quarterly strategic priorities defined before each quarter begins
- Number of qualified opportunities reviewed each month
- Number of opportunities converted into assets, offers, partnerships, or projects
- Revenue influenced by strategic decisions
- Number of systems built that help clients, partners, or projects make money
- Percentage of active work aligned with top strategic priorities
- Progress toward managing larger project budgets, teams, and distribution channels

## Operating Procedures

1. Run a quarterly strategy review before each new quarter.
2. Maintain an opportunity pipeline with people, products, culture, and partnership categories.
3. Score each opportunity by value, audience need, monetization potential, asset potential, and relationship quality.
4. Choose no more than three strategic priorities per quarter.
5. Create a written thesis for each priority: why now, why us, who benefits, and how money is created.
6. Translate each priority into projects, owners, deadlines, and measurable outcomes.
7. Review active projects weekly and remove work that does not support the strategy.
8. After each project, extract reusable assets: playbooks, templates, case studies, datasets, contacts, or content.

## Required Assets

- `annual_strategy.md`: yearly strategic thesis
- `quarterly_priorities.md`: top priorities and success metrics
- `market_map.md`: important industries, communities, platforms, and buyer groups
- `opportunity_pipeline.md`: evaluated opportunities and next actions
- `decision_principles.md`: rules for saying yes or no
- `money_system_map.md`: how each project helps someone earn, save, or multiply money
- `asset_strategy.md`: plan for turning work into reusable leverage

## AI Automation Opportunities

- Score and rank opportunities from notes, emails, and research
- Generate market maps from research inputs
- Detect strategic drift by comparing active tasks against quarterly priorities
- Create strategy memos from raw ideas
- Turn project retrospectives into reusable playbooks
- Monitor competitors, creators, products, and cultural spaces for strategic signals
