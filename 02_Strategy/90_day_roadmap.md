# 90-Day Roadmap to 10 Million Yen Monthly Profit

## Strategic Goal

Reach 10 million yen monthly profit while building long-term assets that make PN_AI_SYSTEM more valuable, repeatable, and scalable.

The business philosophy is the operating filter:

- Help valuable people, products, and culture reach the people who need them.
- Build systems that help others make money.
- Create assets instead of only trading time.
- Become a producer capable of creating and managing 100 million yen projects.

## Current Baseline

Based on the current sample operating data:

- Monthly revenue: 1,050,000 yen
- Monthly profit: 760,000 yen
- Gross margin: 72.4%
- Active services: SNS operation, recruiting support, video production
- Current bottlenecks: low project volume, low average project value, manual reporting, revision risk, limited sales pipeline, limited delivery bench

The 10 million yen monthly profit target requires a step change, not incremental improvement. The next 90 days should focus on creating a small number of high-margin, high-ticket offers supported by reusable assets and AI-assisted delivery.

## Priority Ranking Method

Each priority is ranked by impact and urgency.

- Impact: direct effect on profit, asset creation, delivery capacity, and 100 million yen project readiness
- Urgency: whether it must happen early to unlock later work
- Rank: P0 is immediate, P1 is next, P2 is important but should not distract from P0/P1

- Monthly producer role for founders, creators, brands, and cultural projects
- Scope: positioning, campaign direction, content system, sales assets, partner strategy, monthly KPI review

Why this matters:

- Fastest path to recurring profit
- Matches the producer identity
- Creates case studies, templates, content, and reusable systems
- Builds readiness for larger 100 million yen projects

Weekly actions:

- Build a list of 50 high-fit prospects

- Price: 200,000-300,000 yen

Why this matters:

- High-margin and fast to deliver
- Creates a front-end offer for AI support
- Produces client-specific roadmaps that naturally lead to larger builds
- PN can reuse the same diagnostic system internally

Weekly actions:

- Create diagnostic questionnaire
- Create scoring model
- Create report template
- Pitch to existing clients and warm leads

### P0: Upsell Existing and Warm Clients

Targets:

- SNS operation clients: add short video, monthly campaign, LP, sales content, or AI reporting

- Video clients: add monthly operation, distribution, analysis, additional cuts, or content calendar
- All clients: add AI diagnostic and monthly improvement report

Weekly actions:

- Review every active and past client
- Identify one money-making improvement per client
- Send one specific add-on proposal per client
- Track proposal amount, expected profit, decision date, and next step

## 5. Weekly Operating Cadence

### Monday: Profit and Pipeline

- Review monthly profit target and current booked profit
- Review sales pipeline by stage
- Identify this week's highest-value closes
- Assign owner and next action for every active opportunity
- Send overdue follow-ups

### Tuesday: Sales and Offers

- Run outreach block
- Hold sales calls
- Draft and send proposals
- Improve one offer asset: deck, pricing page, case study, proposal section

### Wednesday: Delivery and Client Money Systems

- Review active client projects
- Identify how each project helps the client make money, save time, recruit better, or reach more people
- Resolve blockers, waiting tasks, and revision risks
- Update project dashboard and client next actions

### Thursday: Asset and AI Build

- Build or improve one reusable asset
- Build or improve one AI workflow
- Convert project notes into templates, prompts, reports, or case studies
- Test automation against real project data

### Friday: Review and Amplification

- Publish one proof-of-work or insight asset
- Send client progress updates
- Review KPI movement
- Decide next week's top 3 priorities
- Archive lessons learned into the knowledge base
